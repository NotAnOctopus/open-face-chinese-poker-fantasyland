Open Face Chinese Poker Fantasyland Thingy-Thing

ok so how this works is this. if you're playing open face chinese poker and you're in fantasyland and you're having an existential crisis about how to arrange your cards, this site is for you. click on 13 cards (for classic ofc) or 14 cards (for pineapple ofc) and hit submit, and it'll tell you how best to arrange your cards for maximum royalties.

i really should add a feature that makes it take into account repeat fantasylands, but i don't know how much they're worth so i'll do that when i find it out. think it's worth like 8.5 in classic and like 7 in pineapple.

13-card version takes like a second to compute, but the 14-card version takes like 20 seconds, so if you're doing the pineapple one and your browser tells you that a script is making it slow down, make it continue and you'll get your answer soon enough.
